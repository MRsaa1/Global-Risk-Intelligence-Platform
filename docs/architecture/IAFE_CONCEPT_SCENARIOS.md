# IAFE — Concept and Scenarios Only (No Implementation Commitment)

**Document purpose:** Describe IAFE (Identifier/Concept as in strategic roadmap) as **scenarios and concept only**. There is **no commitment to implementation** in the codebase.

---

## Scope (Conceptual)

IAFE is referenced in the Phase 2–4 roadmap as a long-term or meta concept. This document captures possible scenarios and use cases for traceability only. No API, no module, no UI, no data model.

---

## Example Scenario (Illustrative)

- **Scenario A:** Long-horizon identity and continuity of critical institutional knowledge across regime or infrastructure transitions.
- **Scenario B:** Cross-domain aggregation of existential-risk indicators with human veto and governance-bound updates.

These are **not** product requirements. They are placeholder narratives for future alignment with governance and research.

---

## Limitation

**No implementation commitment.** The platform does not implement IAFE. No code, no endpoints, no database tables, no UI shall be added for IAFE unless explicitly decided in a future plan.
