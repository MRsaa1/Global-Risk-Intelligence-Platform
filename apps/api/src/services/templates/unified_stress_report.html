Methodology

Risk Scoring Formula

Risk Score = Probability × Impact × Urgency × Confidence

Probability: 0–100% (event likelihood) · Impact: 1–10 (consequence severity) · Urgency: 0.5–2.0 (temporal multiplier) · Confidence: 0.5–1.0 (data quality)
