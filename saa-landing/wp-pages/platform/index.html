Platform

SAA Alliance is a Risk & Intelligence Operating System: six production modules on dedicated domains — risk.saa-alliance.com (Global Risk), analyzer.saa-alliance.com (Risk Analyzer), arin.saa-alliance.com (ARIN), invest.saa-alliance.com (Investment), crypto.saa-alliance.com (Crypto), news.saa-alliance.com (News). Built for banks, insurers, asset managers, and regulators requiring transparent, auditable, real-time analytics. NVIDIA NIM, NeMo Guardrails, and GNN in production.

Core engine

ARIN Platform — multi-agent AI council with 13 specialized agents (Credit, Market, Operational, Liquidity, Regulatory, Systemic, Narrative, Counterparty, Model Risk, ESG, Physical Asset, Sentinel, Ethicist). Single explainable verdict (BUY/SELL/HOLD/AVOID) with consensus, dissent, and confidence. Meta-Decision Governor, Entity Memory, Learning Agent. Full audit trail for regulators.
