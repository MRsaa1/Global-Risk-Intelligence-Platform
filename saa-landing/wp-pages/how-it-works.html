Workflow (6 steps)

1

Discover

Choose a platform/module under Platform based on your domain: risk, news, crypto, liquidity, trading research.

2

Ingest

Ingest market, on-chain and news flows; map them to entities and portfolios for analytics.

3

Analyze

Run quantitative analytics: VaR/CVaR, stress testing, Monte Carlo simulations (10k–100k paths), factor decomposition.

4

Reason (ARIN)

Use multi-agent AI reasoning (6 specialized agents) for predictive risk intelligence and dependency-based exploration.

5

Report

Generate institutional-grade research and analytics outputs (auditable, regulator-aware, non-advisory).

6

Optimize

Scale performance with GPU-ready workloads (LLM inference, GNN, Monte Carlo) using NVIDIA technologies.
