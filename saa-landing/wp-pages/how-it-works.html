Workflow (6 steps)

1

Discover

Choose a module under Platform: Global Risk, Risk Analyzer, ARIN, Investment, Crypto, or News — each on its own dedicated domain.
2

Ingest

Ingest market, on-chain and news flows; map them to entities and portfolios for analytics.
3

Analyze

Run quantitative analytics: VaR/CVaR, stress testing, Monte Carlo simulations (10k–100k paths), factor decomposition.
4

Reason (ARIN)

13 specialized agents deliver one explainable verdict (BUY/SELL/HOLD/AVOID) with consensus, dissent, and confidence. Predictive risk intelligence and dependency-based exploration; full audit trail for regulators.
5

Report

Generate institutional-grade research and analytics outputs (auditable, regulator-aware, non-advisory).
6

Optimize

NVIDIA in production: NIM API, NeMo Guardrails, GNN; GPU workloads (LLM inference, Monte Carlo via cuDF/CVXPY).
