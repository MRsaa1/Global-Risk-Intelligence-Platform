What is Scientific Analytics Alliance?

Scientific Analytics Alliance (SAA) is an integrated ecosystem of enterprise-grade analytics platforms designed for institutional investors, global banks, financial institutions, regulators, and governments. Our unified infrastructure provides comprehensive coverage across traditional finance, digital assets, risk management, and physical economy modeling.

Who can use SAA platforms?

SAA platforms are designed for global systemically important banks (G-SIBs), regional banks, insurance companies, asset managers, infrastructure funds, REITs, family offices, hedge funds, and government agencies requiring enterprise-grade analytics, risk management, and regulatory compliance capabilities.

How do I get access to the platforms?

You can request access by using the Contact page. Public access is provided for analytical demonstration and evaluation purposes. Execution and write operations are restricted to authorized users.

What technologies power SAA platforms?

All platforms share a common technology foundation: FastAPI (Python) backend, React/TypeScript frontend, PostgreSQL/PostGIS for spatial data, Neo4j for graph relationships, Redis for caching, and real-time WebSocket synchronization. We integrate NVIDIA AI technologies including Earth‑2, PhysicsNeMo, and LLM for advanced analytics and simulations.

Are the platforms production-ready?

Yes, platforms operate in production and development modes. Production-ready infrastructure includes Docker orchestration, monitoring, audit logging, and enterprise security standards. Public access is provided for analytical demonstration and evaluation purposes.

What is the pricing model?

Pricing is customized based on your organization’s needs, usage volume, and specific requirements. Please contact us through the Contact page to discuss options tailored to your institution.
