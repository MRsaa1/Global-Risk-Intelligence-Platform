Core capabilities

Quant & risk analytics

  • Portfolio risk metrics (VaR / CVaR) and scenario analysis.
  • Monte Carlo simulations (10k–100k paths) for stress testing and forecasting.
  • Factor decomposition and structured risk attribution.
  • Cross-market and cross-asset correlation / contagion analytics.

ARIN (13 agents)

  • 13 agents: Credit, Market, Operational, Liquidity, Regulatory, Systemic, Narrative, Counterparty, Model Risk, ESG, Physical Asset, Sentinel, Ethicist.
  • One explainable verdict: BUY/SELL/HOLD/AVOID; Governor, Entity Memory, Learning Agent.
  • Decision Objects and full audit trail for verifiability and professional review.

News Intelligence

  • 12 risk agents; RiskMirror; Headline-to-PnL.
  • NIM (Llama 3.3 70B); Impact Graph.
  • Dashboards for monitoring and operational workflows.

Digital assets analytics

  • Multi-metrics (6 categories); rating v1.4; Risk & Intelligence OS v2.
  • Send to ARIN; Report Orchestrator.
  • NIM (DeepSeek R1, Llama 8B); analyst dashboards for exploration and reporting.
