Core capabilities

Quant & risk analytics

  • Portfolio risk metrics (VaR / CVaR) and scenario analysis.
  • Monte Carlo simulations (10k–100k paths) for stress testing and forecasting.
  • Factor decomposition and structured risk attribution.
  • Cross-market and cross-asset correlation / contagion analytics.

AI research automation (ARIN)

  • Multi-agent architecture (6 specialized agents) for research workflows.
  • Structured outputs: summaries, evidence trails, and analyst-ready narratives.
  • Monitoring support: detect shifts, generate alerts, and propose next steps.
  • Designed to support verifiability and professional review.

Information intelligence

  • News analytics with multilingual support and narrative exploration.
  • Entity-centric trend tracking and signal extraction.
  • Dashboards for monitoring and operational workflows.

Digital assets analytics

  • On-chain analytics for institutional-grade monitoring.
  • Market + chain correlation and risk diagnostics.
  • Analyst dashboards for exploration and reporting.

Frontend / UX
